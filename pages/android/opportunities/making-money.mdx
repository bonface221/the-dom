## 1. Freelancing

Freelancing is a great way to make money as an Android Developer. You can work on your own time and you can work on projects that you are interested in. You can also work on
projects on the side while you are working a full-time job.

There are many Freelancing platforms you can leverage and find work on. e.g

1. [Upwork](https://www.upwork.com/)
2. [Freelancer](https://www.freelancer.com/)
3. [Fiverr](https://www.fiverr.com/)
4. [Toptal](https://www.toptal.com/)
5. [Guru](https://www.guru.com/)
6. [PeoplePerHour](https://www.peopleperhour.com/)

## 2. Youtube Channel / Blogging

You can make money by creating a Youtube Channel or a Blog. You can create content around Android Development and make money through Ads or Affiliate Marketing, sponsored posts, promotions.
Youtubing and Blogging is a great way to make passive income. You can create content once and make money from it for a long time.

## 3. Contributing to Open Source

You can make money by contributing to Open Source. You can contribute to Open Source projects and get paid for it. There are many companies that pay developers to contribute to Open Source projects.
